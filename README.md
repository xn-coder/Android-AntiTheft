## ✨ Features

Android-AntiTheft comes packed with features designed to give you peace of mind:

*   🔒 **Remote Lock & Wipe:** Securely lock your device or erase all personal data remotely if it's lost or stolen, protecting your privacy.
*   📍 **Location Tracking:** Pinpoint your device's exact location on a map, helping you recover it quickly.
*   🚨 **Intruder Selfie:** Automatically capture a photo of anyone attempting to unlock your device with incorrect credentials and send it to your trusted contact.
*   🗣️ **Scream Alarm:** Trigger a loud, piercing alarm remotely to help you locate a misplaced device, even if it's on silent.
*   🛡️ **SIM Change Detection:** Get instant notifications when the SIM card in your device is changed, indicating potential unauthorized access.
*   📞 **Trusted Contacts:** Configure a list of trusted phone numbers that can send commands to your device and receive security alerts.
