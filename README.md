# 🚀 Android-AntiTheft

Lost your phone? Don't panic! 🛡️ Android-AntiTheft is a robust application designed to help you protect your device from theft and recover it with intelligent, remote-controlled features.

## ✨ Features

*   🌍 **Remote Location Tracking:** Pinpoint your device's last known location on a map.
*   🚨 **Loud Siren Activation:** Trigger a piercing alarm remotely, even if your phone is on silent mode.
*   📸 **Intruder Selfie:** Automatically capture a photo of anyone trying to unlock your device with an incorrect password.
*   🚫 **SIM Card Change Alert:** Get notified immediately if your SIM card is replaced.
*   🔒 **Remote Device Locking:** Lock your phone instantly to prevent unauthorized access.
*   🗑️ **Data Wipe (Optional):** Erase sensitive data remotely as a last resort.

## ▶️ Usage

Once installed, follow these steps to configure and use Android-AntiTheft:

1.  **Grant Permissions:** On first launch, the app will request necessary permissions (e.g., location, camera, SMS, device administrator). Grant all of them for full functionality.
2.  **Set Up Anti-Theft Guardians:** Register trusted phone numbers or email addresses that can send commands to your device or receive alerts.
3.  **Configure Features:** Enable/disable features like Intruder Selfie, SIM Change Alert, and set up your remote commands.
4.  **Remote Control:** Use predefined SMS commands (e.g., `ANTITHEFT_LOCATE`, `ANTITHEFT_SIREN`) from a registered guardian number to trigger actions on your lost device. *Refer to the in-app guide for a full list of commands.*
